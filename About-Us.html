Tecknel

Tecknel.lk was incorporated & established in 2007, and to date is a well-known leading retail store. With lot of effort and dedication the management desired to have a multi branded laptop outlet in one roof only then Tecknel (Pvt) was established as the multi branded retail store in the Island in 2012 and of course the first of its kind.

This is a one stop shop for all your computer needs with an outstanding range of Notebooks, Branded PCs, Computer Accessories. Comprising of the world's leading IT Brands such as HP, Asus, Dell, Lenovo and Acer to name a few, its display layout is designed and branded to precise specification by each of the relevant manufacturers.

One of the outstanding features of our service is our technically qualified staff, who educate our customers on the technical features of products in a simple manner while guiding them into making the right choice as per their budget & requirement.

Giving emphasis to value for money & being the benchmark of the industry we at Tecknel now offer our customers with a unique after sales service support for free the 2nd and 3rd year for all our laptops.
